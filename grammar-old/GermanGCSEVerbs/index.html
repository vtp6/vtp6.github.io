VTP6 Grammar - German Verbs

Present

Translation: I do



Past

Translation: I did / I had done (pluperfect)


Note: In the perfect tense, use sein instead of haben if the verb is movement.


Future

Translation: I will do



Conditional

Translation: I would do



Passive

Translation: I am done (present) / I was done (past)
